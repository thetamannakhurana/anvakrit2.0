Who Can Participate

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you qualify for participation in Anvakrit 2.0

Category I - Students

  • Grades 11 & 12 students
  • Undergraduate students (all streams)
  • Valid school/college ID required
  • No prior forensic knowledge needed
  • Must form teams of 4 before registration

Category II - Advanced

  • Postgraduate & Doctoral students
  • Professional experts in relevant fields
  • Fields: Forensics, Criminology, Law, Psychology, Cyber Security
  • Valid institutional ID required
  • Individual or team registration (5 members)

General Requirements

  • Must be Indian resident
  • Age: 16+ years
  • Fluency in English (written & spoken)
  • Ability to attend offline rounds at NFSU Delhi
  • Generic devices (access to mobile phones and laptops, at least 1 per team)
  • No past criminal record
