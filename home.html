About The Event

Beyond the Crime Scene

After the resounding success of its first edition, Anvakrit 2.0 returns with greater depth and tougher challenges

This year, every clue counts — every trace, every click, every move could unravel the mystery. Centered around Digital Forensics, participants will step beyond the crime scene, navigating the digital world with investigative acumen in dynamic, real-world scenarios designed to test your limits.

🎯

Mission

Test your investigative skills through multi-scene investigations with domain-integrated challenges in real-world crime scenarios.

🏆

Competition Format

Two parallel tracks for different expertise levels with exciting prizes totaling ₹2.4 Lakh across both categories.

🎓

Experience & Learning

Held at NFSU Delhi Campus with state-of-the-art facilities, workshops, and immersive crime scene challenges.

Rules & Guidelines

Competition Rules

Important guidelines and regulations for all participants

🔒 General Rules

  • Valid institutional/school ID card mandatory for offline rounds
  • Registration is non-refundable once confirmed
  • No changes in team names or composition after registration
  • Each team member must register individually
  • Surprise elements throughout the competition starting from registration

🎯 Competition Conduct

  • Maintain integrity and professionalism throughout
  • Follow chain of custody protocols during evidence handling
  • Respect crime scene boundaries and markers
  • No use of unauthorized external assistance or resources
  • Decisions of judges and organizers are final

📋 What's Included

  • Lunch and dinner during the event
  • Registration kits and materials
  • Workshops and expert lectures
  • Lab visits at NFSU Delhi Campus
  • Certificate of participation
  • Optional: Accommodation at ₹500/day (includes breakfast)

🏆 Prize Pool

  • Category I: Top 3 teams share ₹1.2 Lakh
  • Category II: Top 2 teams share ₹1.2 Lakh
  • Total Prize Pool: ₹2.4 Lakh
  • Special recognition for outstanding performance
